StreetHub Code of Conduct

Keep StreetHub a safe and welcoming space for everyone

Keep StreetHub Anonymous

StreetHub is an anonymous space for people to share their thoughts and express themselves. Let's keep it that way: do not share any personal information or the identities of others in your posts.

Do not discriminate

We do not tolerate racism, hateful language or discrimination of any kind.

Do not use profane language

Do not use any profane, explicit or offensive language when writing a StreetHub post.

Only leave positive or constructive comments

Don't have anything nice to say? Don't comment it! Comments should be supportive of the user, or feature constructive criticism only.

Articles must not contain explicit or graphic content

Keep StreetHub friendly for all users. Posts should not contain violent, offensive or NFSW content.
